Drag and drop:

The Dashboard tab can be moved among tab groups using drag and drop.

Filtering:

To display filter criteria click the "Filter" checkbox (in the bottom right tab labeled "Table"). The Plus button adds a new filter, the Minus buttons remove a filter. To turn off filtering, click the Close button, or remove all the filters (using the Minus buttons), or uncheck the "Filter" checkbox.

Platform Status Detail Views:

Sensor number 5 in the Engineering Sensors tab has an issue; to drill-down into the data, open a detail view for the sensor by clicking the gray arrow to the left of the circular red P indicator. To close the detail view, click the gray arrow again.

To change the detail view, click the Panel Controls button in the upper right of the Engineering Sensors tab, then select a view from the drop-down menu.

Click and drag on the Search Map (upper left) to expand it
Panel Editing:

To edit the Information panel, hover your mouse over it, then click the button that appears in the panel's upper right. To exit edit mode, click the Cancel button.

Panel Change Views:

To change the view of the Documentation subpanel of Panel D, hover your mouse over the subpanel, then click the button that appears and click again on the drop-down menu (for this demo it doesn't matter which menu item you choose). The subpanel will change to a detail view. To close the detail view, click the Cancel button.
